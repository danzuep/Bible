31After King Jehoiachin of Judah had been in prison in Babylon for almost thirty-seven years, Evil-Merodach became the king of Babylon. That was on March 31 of the year that Evil-Merodach became king. 32He always spoke kindly to Jehoiachin and gave him a position in which he was honored more than all the other kings who had been exiled/taken to Babylon. 33He supplied new clothes for Jehoiachin, to replace the clothes that he had been wearing in prison.
